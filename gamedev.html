My Interest in Game Development 🎮

I am highly interested in open-world games where players have the freedom to explore, interact, and experience the game world without strict limitations. I enjoy games that allow creativity, discovery, and immersion, where anything and everything feels possible.

I am especially fascinated by the idea of creating a deep-dive experience — a system where players are not just playing the game, but truly feel like they are inside it. This goes beyond traditional gameplay and moves towards something similar to virtual reality, but even more immersive, where the environment, movement, and interactions feel realistic and engaging.

Games like Wuthering Waves and Black Myth: Wukong inspire me because of their world design, fluid combat systems, and immersive environments. These experiences motivate me to explore how such systems can be created and improved further.

What I Want to Create

My goal is to develop a highly immersive open-world experience where players can freely explore and interact with the environment. I want to focus on creating depth — not just visually, but also through gameplay, emotions, and player choices.

I aim to build systems that make players feel present inside the game, almost like a “deep dive” experience where the line between reality and the game world feels blurred.

Game Ideas

  • A large open-world RPG with choice-based storylines
  • A deep exploration game where players uncover hidden lore
  • A survival-based open-world with dynamic environments

Skills I Want to Learn

  • Game engines (Unity / Unreal Engine)
  • C# / C++ for game development
  • Game physics and animation systems
  • Artificial Intelligence in games
